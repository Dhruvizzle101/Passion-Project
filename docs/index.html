About Apple Inc.

Apple Inc. designs, manufactures, and markets smartphones, personal computers, tablets, wearables, and accessories worldwide. The company offers iPhone, Mac, iPad, and wearables, home, and accessories. It also provides AppleCare and Apple Services. In addition, Apple sells various third-party products and accessories.

Future Plans

Apple is focusing on expanding its services business, developing AI capabilities for its devices, and exploring new product categories like AR/VR with the Apple Vision Pro. The company continues to invest in environmental initiatives, aiming for carbon neutrality across its supply chain by 2030.
